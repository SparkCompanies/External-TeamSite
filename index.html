Getting Started
Welcome to Spark.

Everything you need to know before your first day. Complete your onboarding steps and you'll be cleared to start on time.

Your Onboarding Checklist
What to expect in your inbox

After your placement is confirmed, our HR team will send you everything electronically. Here's what's coming and what you need to do.

STEP 1

Check Your Email

You'll receive an email from sterlingtmx@sparktalentinc.com containing two important links. This email may take up to one business day after your placement is confirmed.

Don't see it? Check your spam/junk folder — emails from Salesforce sometimes get filtered.

STEP 2

Complete Greenshades Onboarding Paperwork

The first link takes you to Greenshades, our payroll system. You'll complete your tax forms (W-4), direct deposit setup, I-9 employment verification, emergency contacts, and personal information — all online.

W-4 Tax Forms Direct Deposit I-9 Verification Emergency Contact
STEP 3

Sign Your Offer Letter

The second link in the same email is your offer letter, generated through Conga. Review the details — pay rate, position, start date — and sign electronically. This is a separate step from the Greenshades paperwork.

STEP 4

Background Check & Drug Test

You'll receive a separate background check invitation — please complete it promptly. If your position requires a drug test, you'll also receive a drug test form with instructions. Both are standard and required before your start date.

STEP 5

You're Cleared — Get Ready for Day 1!

Once all steps are completed and your background check clears, your recruiter will confirm your official start date. You're all set!

FAQ
Quick answers to common questions.
01When do I get paid?+
Every Friday. If a holiday falls on Friday, pay may shift to Thursday. Check the GreenEmployee portal or app for your latest paystub.
02How do I view my paystubs?+
Log in to GreenEmployee.com or use the GreenEmployee mobile app. You can view current and past pay statements along with tax details.
03How do I update my direct deposit?+
Log in to the GreenEmployee app or portal → Direct Deposit section. You can add, edit, or remove bank accounts. Changes may take one pay cycle to take effect.
04Where do I find my W-2?+
W-2s are available in the GreenEmployee portal and app. Contact HR if you need login help.
05How do I change my address?+
Update through the GreenEmployee app or portal under personal info. This keeps your tax withholdings and mailings accurate.
06How do I download the GreenEmployee app?+
Search "GreenEmployee" (one word) by Greenshades Software in the App Store or Google Play. Use company code sparkportfolio. Do not download "Spark Group."
07My paycheck seems wrong — what do I do?+
Contact Payroll immediately at Timecards@sparktalentinc.com or call (586) 930-5000. Have your paystub ready so we can investigate quickly.
08How do I submit my timecard?+
Email timecards to Timecards@sparktalentinc.com by the deadline from your recruiter or account manager. Late submissions may delay pay.
09Who handles benefits enrollment?+
Tamika Coleman, HR Lead & Benefits Advisor. Email tcoleman@sparkcompanies.com or call (586) 930-5000. Benefits administered through HUB International.
10What is the Flex American Worker benefit?+
Spark partners with The American Worker to offer medical, dental, vision, and supplemental coverage to all eligible team members. Visit theamericanworker.com or contact Tamika Coleman in HR for enrollment details and rates.
11How does the referral program work?+
Refer someone to Spark, and once they're hired and complete 520 hours, you'll receive a $100 Amazon gift card. Your referral gets $50 too. Submit referrals through your recruiter — they'll track eligibility and notify you when the gift card is on the way.
